Our Story

Empowering students with innovative project ideas since 2024

Why We Started

We noticed a gap in the market for a platform that connects students with innovative project ideas and resources.

Our journey began when four passionate individuals came together with a shared vision: to create a platform that would help students find inspiration and guidance for their academic projects. We understand the challenges students face when looking for project ideas, and we're here to make that process easier.
